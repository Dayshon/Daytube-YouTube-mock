This video is the audible version of the book "Self Discipline Audibook", this is for those people that don't have the desired time to read but do have the time to listen.Self-discipline is about maintaining integrity between yourself and your own personal promises. This one simple thing: getting up when you intend to get up—and doing it the first time—is both a small win and a big win at once… It's a small win because it's a small and simple action: alarm goes off, you get up.
